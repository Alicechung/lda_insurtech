Free App Anyone?

February 3, 2017

iOasys and The Bakery are partnering to debut the next big startup competition for mobile, the App Factor. The two stage competition format will showcase the best new mobile projects and ideas coming from across the UK. On 14th March 2017, in the grand final, entrepreneurs will pitch to investors and guests and compete to have their mobile app business developed for FREE by iOasys.
