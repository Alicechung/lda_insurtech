Telkonet Announces Hiring Of Roth Capital Partners To Explore Strategic Alternatives

12 Jul 2018

Telkonet, Inc. (OTCQB:TKOI) (the “Company” or “Telkonet”), creator of the EcoSmart platform of intelligent automation solutions designed to optimize comfort, energy efficiency and operational analytics in support of the emerging Internet of Things (IoT), today announced that it has hired the investment banking firm of Roth Capital Partners, LLC to explore strategic alternatives with a view to enhancing shareholder value. No assurance can be given that this process will result in a transaction or, if a transaction is undertaken, the timing or the terms of any such transaction.
