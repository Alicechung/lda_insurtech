The Industrial Internet Consortium Publishes The Data Edition Of The Journal Of Innovation

29 Jun 2018

Leveraging data analytics to achieve better business outcomes
The Industrial Internet Consortium® (IIC™), the world’s leading organization transforming business and society by accelerating the adoption of the Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT), published the eighth edition of the Journal of Innovation focused entirely on data. The issue shows why it is so important for industrial organizations to leverage data analytics for better business decisions.
Edy Liongosari
“Data is becoming a strategic business asset for many organizations, especially those involved in Industrial IoT,” said Edy Liongosari, Co-chair of the IIC Thought Leadership Task Group and Chief Research Scientist at Accenture Labs. “In this edition of the Journal of Innovation, IIC members show how data and analytics can drive innovation, reduce costs and transform businesses.”
